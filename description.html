Description

An overview of the FJs555 project.

Purposes

FJs555 will

  • Discuss the purpose of fighter aircraft and their roles in air defense.
  • Analyze United States fighter aircraft and compare characteristics and specifications.
  • Discuss new air defense programs from the United States Air Force and others.

Fighter Aircraft to be Discussed

  • American Aircraft

    • F-35 Lightning II -- modern
    • F-22 Raptor -- modern
    • F-18 Hornet -- current
    • F-16 Falcon -- current
    • F-15 Eagle -- current
    • F-14 Tomcat -- past

  • Non-American Aircraft

    • J-20
    • Su-57
    • FC-31
    • Saab JAS 39
    • Eurofighter Typhoon
    • Dassault Rafale
    • MiG-29
    • Su-27

  • Future Aircraft/Aircraft Prototypes

    • Next Generation Air Dominance (NGAD) fighter
    • Su-75
    • RQ-3 Darkstar (though not a fighter aircraft)
